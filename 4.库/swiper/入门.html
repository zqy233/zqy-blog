modules #

  1. 导入模块import { EffectCards } from "swiper"
  2. 导入样式import "swiper/css/effect-cards"
  3. 绑定模块:modules="[EffectCards]"

有以下effect属性

sh
- EffectFade fade
- EffectCube cube
- EffectCoverflow coverflow
- EffectFlip flip
- EffectCards cards
- EffectCreative creative
